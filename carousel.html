About Us

Daping’s Food Bistro has started at the 4th quarter of year 2022 and fully established this year 2023. The name itself comes from the nickname of the one and only daughter of the owners. It’s a dream restaurant for the couple.

At “Daping’s Food Bistro”, we wish to offer our personal rendition of mix Asian and Southern cuisine with an innovative gastronomic take on traditional cooking methods, fine produce and the legacy of our shared food heritage. Moreover, our mission is to be the most sustainable restaurant in the Philippines by sourcing our ingredients locally, supplementing produce herbs grown on our own garden and giving back to the community through urban farming education. We guarantee that the freshness of our food is roaring high than standards.
